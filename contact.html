About Osterhaus Academy

Osterhaus Academy (Osterhaus Scholarium of Alpha) is an educational and research-focused organization dedicated to alpha generation, quantitative finance, systematic investing, artificial intelligence in finance, and long-term capital development.

The academy was established to explore financial market behavior, pricing inefficiencies, and modern investment frameworks through structured research and educational initiatives.

Through educational resources, research publications, and financial learning programs, Osterhaus Academy aims to help individuals better understand modern financial markets and develop informed decision-making capabilities.

Core areas of research include Alpha Generation, Market Structure, Systematic Investing, Compounding Growth, Artificial Intelligence in Finance, Portfolio Construction, and Risk Management.
